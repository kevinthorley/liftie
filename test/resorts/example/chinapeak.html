Conditions

China Peak is closed Monday through Friday and will reopen on Saturday, December 12th.   We are expecting snow later this week and will update with open runs and lifts for the upcoming weekend. 

We will be opening runs that have only natural snow, so while the surface is good, the coverage is very thin, so ski and ride with much care, always ski and ride with friends. Get the most up-to-date info at THE LATEST or the China Peak snow phone at 559.233.3330.
